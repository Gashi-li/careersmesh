About

Career intelligence, organized for clarity.

CareersMesh is an AI-powered career intelligence platform designed to make job market signals easier to understand through structured views by major, role, and region.

What it is

A career insight platform focused on turning fragmented job market information into more useful and readable signals.

Why it exists

Career information is often scattered, outdated, or too broad to support real decision-making.

What it aims to do

Help users understand where opportunities are emerging, how demand is changing, and what that means for next steps.
